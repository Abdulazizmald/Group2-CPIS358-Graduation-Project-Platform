About Us

We are students of CPIS-358 creating this platform for helping other fellow students find the correct criteria and browse different approved projects.

Our Mission

To streamline the graduation project selection process by providing a centralized platform where students can:

  • Browse previously approved projects for inspiration
  • Find team members with complementary skills
  • Submit proposals with proper guidance
  • Receive feedback from faculty advisors
