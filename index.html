The Problem

Students struggle to find suitable graduation project ideas and compatible team members. Many waste weeks brainstorming, propose unoriginal ideas that have been done before. There's no centralized system to browse past projects, assess wanted criteria. This results in project delays, conflicts, and students settling for mediocre ideas instead of pursuing innovative work.

The Solution

A web platform where students can browse past and proposed graduation projects, find team members, and build proposals collaboratively. Students can search projects by category/technology, bookmark ideas, and get feedback from faculty advisors.
